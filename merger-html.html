This database contains all award winners for the awards MVP of the Year, Practice Groups of the Year, Rising Stars and Titans of the Plaintiffs Bar. The oldest of these awards dates to 2010. Awards are displayed in the database with the name of the firm at the time the award was won.

Post-merger awards are not combined with pre-merger awards in the generated data visualizations. A single award for a firm can be associated with multiple practice tags, so the combined values of each bar in the bar chart can exceed the total number of awards won by the firm. However, no single bar can exceed the total number of awards won by the firm.
